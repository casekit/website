Case Kit is case management software built for community and social organisations

How Case Kit can support your organisation

A powerful toolkit

Support the whole of your organisation with powerful analytics, report generation, appointment reminders, referral letter templates, and more.

A resource and services library

Keep track of the resources and services you use and signpost people to. Discover new resources and make your own services known to more people.

Empowering people

People are more engaged in systems they are a part of. Case Kit allows organisations to give people easy, remote, (and, of course, secure) access to their own records.
