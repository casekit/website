Supporting people who support each other

Case management software built for community and social organisations

Hello there, we are Case Kit

We are a social enterprise building a case management platform for community support and other third sector organisations. Case Kit will have everything you need to create, update, and keep track of cases, alongside analytics and tools to provide further support to your organisation.

We are currently designing, developing, and testing and hope to be launching in Summer 2020. Get in touch to find out more or to register your interest

We want to hear from you

If your work involves case management and you have a spare 10 minutes, please share your insights so we can build case management software that helps support organisations like yours.

Powerful supporting tools

We want our software to work in a holistic way to support the whole of an organisation. We will be adding more and more tools to help do this: powerful analytics, report generation, appointment reminders, referral letter templates, and more.

A shared resource library

We are creating a way for organisations to connect and share resources with each other in a durable and scalable way: a library of resources where organisations can store the resources they use, discover new ones, and share their own services with other organisations.

Empowering users

We believe that people are more engaged in systems they have access to, and more likely to attend appointments or use services they have a saved record of. We plan to give organisations the ability to let people easily, remotely (and, of course, securely) access their own cases.
